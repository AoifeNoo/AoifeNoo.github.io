VFX

We work closely with directors and production to breakdown a script and come up with the best solution to tell the story.

We collaborate with all departments and at all stages of the production including:
 
Concept Design
Visual Effects Design & Supervision
On-Set Supervision
3D Modelling
Compositing
Photogrammetry
