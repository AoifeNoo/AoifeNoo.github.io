About BOWSIE

Bowsie was formed in 2011 by Aoife Noonan and Ben O'Connor with the aim to create a studio where we can see ideas through from concept and fabrication through to post-production all under one roof, with a focus on craft and artistry.

We have gained a reputation for our imaginative approach to our effects work as well as our own music videos and short films, mixing traditional practical techniques and digital technology and have won various awards, including the short film A Terrible Hullabaloo, directed by Ben and written by Aoife.

Based in Harolds Cross, Dublin we are centrally located and always open to having a chat about new projects and collaborations, so don't hesitate to get in touch.
